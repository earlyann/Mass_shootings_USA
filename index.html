Mass Shootings in the USA

1982 - 2023

Gun ownership in the United States is the highest in the world, with an estimated 120.5 firearms per 100 people according to the Small Arms Survey, and is constitutionally protected by the Second Amendment to the United States Constitution. This map shows the horrors that mass shootings have inflicted on our country over the last several decades. Estimates suggest mass shootings make up less than 2% of all gun deaths. This is not to make light of the issue, but rather to highlight the broadness and complexity of the problem of gun violence. Data source: US Mass Shootings 1982-2023.
